Frequently Asked Questions

If you encounter any issues with a transaction, please contact our support team immediately via WhatsApp, phone, or email. Provide details of the transaction and any evidence (screenshots, messages) to help us resolve the issue quickly.

If you suspect fraudulent activity, please contact our safety team immediately at safety@eduvosc2c.co.za or call our emergency line at +27 83 987 6543. Do not proceed with any transactions if you have concerns.

We offer free workshops in various communities. Check our events calendar for upcoming sessions, or contact your nearest community center. You can also request a workshop for your cooperative or market by emailing training@eduvosc2c.co.za.

Verification helps build trust with buyers. You can start the process in your account settings by providing identification and proof of business. Visit one of our community centers for in-person verification, which is often faster. Verification typically takes 2-3 business days.
