About Edu C2C

Empowering South Africa's informal economy through technology

Our Mission

Edu C2C Marketplace was created to bridge the digital divide in South Africa's informal economy. With 18% of GDP and 15.8 million people employed in the informal sector, we recognized a critical need for a platform that serves this vital part of our economy.

Traditional e-commerce platforms often exclude informal traders due to technological barriers, trust issues, and lack of features tailored to their needs. We're changing that by building a platform designed specifically for South Africa's street vendors, small-scale farmers, artisans, and other informal traders.

Our Vision

To create a digitally inclusive marketplace where every informal trader can thrive, expand their customer base, and grow their business beyond local boundaries.

Mobile-First Design

Frequently Asked Questions

Edu C2C is specifically designed for South Africa's informal economy. We offer features like low-data usage, mobile money integration, barter options, and local language support that aren't typically available on mainstream platforms. We also provide digital literacy training to help sellers succeed online.

Basic listings are completely free. We take a small commission (5%) only when you make a sale through our secure payment system. Cash transactions have no fees.

Verification helps build trust with buyers. You can verify your account by providing a copy of your ID and proof of address. Our community team may also schedule a quick video call or in-person meeting at one of our local partner locations.

We recommend using our secure payment system for transactions. For in-person meetings, we suggest choosing public locations. All users can rate and review each other, and we have a reporting system for any issues. We also partner with local community organizations to provide safe trading spaces.

We offer several support options: 1) In-app help center with tutorials, 2) WhatsApp support line, 3) Community workshops in major cities, and 4) Partner locations where you can get in-person assistance. Visit our Contact page for details.
